My name is Kasija
I was brought to the shelter by sanitary services. I was about to be euthanized there. Not becouse I was sick or other reasons, just becouse my time being there has expired. But as it turned out, it was not my time to die. That day as February 13th - I declare it my second birthday!
I came here very scared and traumatized. All my life I have lived in a cage. I was afraid of humans, dogs, sounds, smells, falling leafes, pretty much everything. If you would see me today, you would not believe that I was so frightened.
With a lot of time and work with great people I am a friendly, social, fun and extremely beutiful dog. I learned to sit calmly in car, I know what is taking a ferry boat, chill in a cafe, walk around the city. I saw the sea, fields and met a lot of people and dogs. What was my greatest horror in the past is now my entertainment. I am constantly smiling, ready to pounce into a loving person's hug. Ofcourse, slowly, carefully, so nobody would become upset. Life is brilliant and I love every moment of it.
Oww... I would love to find an owner, who could make my life even more exciting! I am worth it!

My name is Maverikas
Leave the security of your home to me! I feel the need to tell you more about myself.
I am a very honorable dog. Mr. Inteligent, or atleast how my best friend calls me. I am strict for people who do not belong here. And when I say strict, I'm not saying this just for show. I have my rules and I follow them!

  • You can enter my home only with the permission of my owners
  • Don't push your luck with trying to buy me off with snacks or cuteness.
  • Please do not interupt my naps, especially if I do not know you.

But when we are friends, it is completely different!
I am very loyal, friendly, happy and devoted. I am always waiting with a broad smile.
Just one problem - I want to find an owner that is just like me. Intelligent and friendly.

My name is Naina
I know that my previous shelter home are happy to see me finnaly find my own family.
Starting today, I am oficially Kamile's dog! But probably I was already her's when we first met.
Everytime she came to my old home, she would take me to the sea, or to the park or to her work! While at first I was afraid of a lot of things, I am not anymore.
I even managed to find a mutual understanding with Kamile's cat! In the past, I almost knew nothing. But when I met Kamile, I learned a lot of things, becouse an owner with a good heart and a dog can become friends for life!
